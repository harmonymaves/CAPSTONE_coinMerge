How To Play

Game Process

  • Click the jar for coin(s)
  • Merge same coins to make a stack
  • Merge coins to make higher value coins
  • Give customers correct change
  • Earn points from helping customers
  • Upgrade your candy shop
  • Learn while having fun!

Coins to Merge

Coin merges are based on real currency and can merge up to a dollar coin. Merge to make nickels, dimes, quarters, and dollar coin.

Board is Full, Now What?

Click an unwanted coin back into the coin jar OR click the 'Reset Coins' button to fully clear all coins off the boards.

I Have Points, Where Do I Spend Them?

The shop is a great place to spend and make your candy shop cool! The shop is located on your left.
